For the last six months the VIA has been keeping tabs in a situation that's been developing in the region, a local miltia group has been gathering men and supplies in anticipation for a coup attempt on the local government. They lacked the equipment to actually go forward with the coup, but that recently changed in the last week when they recieved a significant equipment and weapons shipment delivered by Groupe Valmont, who are suspected of acting in behalf of another country. Contractors are therefore being hired to destroy the delivered assets to prevent the coup from taking place. A Virelian UAV along with an informant inside the milita were able to provide the composition of the shipment via imaging as well as confirming that Valmont has some operatives on the ground training and aiding the militia. However the informant has since failed to check in so he's being considered MIA, therefore the VIA would like contractors to extract his family from the country before they are killed. Additionally UAV has had to exit the AO upon confirmation of enemy AA, as a result of all of this the exact location of the items from the shipment is unknown.
