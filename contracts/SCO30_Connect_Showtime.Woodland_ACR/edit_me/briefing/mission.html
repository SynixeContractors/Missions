CLF chartered train carrying their new offerings to the Novigrad International Arms Expo, has not reached its destination. It departed Kamyshovo post at 9:35 and has never reached Elektrozavodsk. Subsequently shots were heard on train radio and dispatcher in Elektrozavodsk stopped responding. Police were called to the location but were terribly outgunned and have fallen back to Kamyshovo. Our employer ships the vehicles empty of ammunition and loaded with fuel, but the machinegun and AA missiles can be reloaded in short time. Synixe has been contracted to recapture the experimental tank and wants us to deny assets to the rebels.
