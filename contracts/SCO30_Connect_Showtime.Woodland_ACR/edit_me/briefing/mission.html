The CLF-chartered train carrying their new offerings to the Novigrad International Arms Expo, has not reached its destination. It departed the Kamyshovo post at 9:35 and never reached Elektrozavodsk. Subsequently, shots were heard on the train radio and the dispatcher in Elektrozavodsk stopped responding. Police were called to the location but were terribly outgunned and have fallen back to Kamyshovo. Chernogorsk Locomotive Factory ships the vehicles empty of ammunition and loaded with fuel, but the machine guns and AA missiles can be reloaded in a short time. Synixe has been contracted to recapture the experimental tank and local government wants us to deny assets to the rebels.
