EMPLOYER Name: Chernogorsk Locomotive Factory Heavy industry conglomerate, one of the manufacturers of T-100 tanks, was due to unveil its newest "Futura" railgun turret on a T-100 hull during the Novigrad International Arms Expo. The train carrying their new prototype has gone missing. It was additionally carrying two Wiesel APCs, radar and AA variants. ENEMY FORCES Name: Dzieci Nadchodzącego Świtu Decently equipped rebel force, allegedly supported by Calvane Security Pact with weapons and intelligence about the train. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Chernorussian Police First response force of police is outgunned and has taken fire from direction of Elektrozavodsk and have set up positions in Kamyshovo. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilian life goes on as normal
