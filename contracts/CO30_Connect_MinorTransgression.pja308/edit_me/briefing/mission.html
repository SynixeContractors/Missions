The CFO of Holmium, Ed Zitron, alongside his brother-in-law George, has been visiting the local area to see what the on-the-ground reality of their company is. The corporation usually doubles up on security, relying on local keys of power as well as mercenaries to keep their operation running smoothly. Unknown to the corporation, the Neron Clan, its local partner, secured outside support and turned on the operation, attacking the ION compound. Ed, suffering from malaria, had been resting inside the ION compound and was taken hostage by the attackers. Holmium has entered talks with the Neron Clan for Ed's release. They managed to put a worm on one of the hostage takers' computers that they have not shut down. This gives us a live feed of Ed's current position. While Ed was down with malaria, George was visiting the employee housing in Horasan. There is no information about his current whereabouts.
