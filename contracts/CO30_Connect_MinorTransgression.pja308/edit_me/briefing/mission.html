CFO of Holmium, Ed Zitron alongside his brother-in-law George have been visiting the local area to see what the on-the-ground reality of their company is. The corporation usually doubles up on the security, relying on local keys of power as well as merceneries to keep their operation running smoothly. Unknown to the corporation, Neron clan which has been their local partner has secured outside support and turned on the operation attacking the ION compound. Ed suffering from malaria has been resting inside the ION compound and was taken hostage by the attackers. Holmium has entered in talks with Neron for the release of Ed. They have managed to put a worm on one of the hostage takers computer they have not shut down. This gives us a life feed on Ed's current position. While Ed was down with maleria, George was visiting the employee housing in Horasan. There are no information on his current whereabouts.
