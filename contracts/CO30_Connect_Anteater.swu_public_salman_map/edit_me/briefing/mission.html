Yesterday after heavy fighting inside the town Her Majesty's forces have been beaten back and were forced to withdraw. While retreating, a Pandur IFV has struck an anti-tank mine wounding its crew and passengers, during the ensuing firefight, a Leopard tank "Anteater" was struck in the left track by an RPG, breaking it, the tank turned sharply and the other track walked off. Unable to move and with many wounded among accompanying infantry they were surrounded and cut off. As the gunfight raged into the night, immediate risks were dealt with, but with approaching daylight, it is certain their position is in grave danger. During the night a supply drop from a helicopter has been shot down with shoulder launched surface-to-air missile and crashed into a nearby building. With Khaybaran forces stretched thin over the frontline, the stricken "Anteater" and its escort need rescuing from their position deep in the town. With dawn soon approaching and reserves still hours away they require assistance. Synixe has been ordered to rescue the tank and its escort.
