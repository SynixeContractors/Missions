Contractors have been hired by Vrana Corpration to assist in the transport of food and medical supplies to designated IDAP camps in order to assist the locals. The situation in greater Takistan is tense, and many locals have begun to arm themselves with surplus equipment to defend themselves and their homes after recent pullouts. IDAP has entered the scene in an attempt to help out those in need by providing food and medical supplies. EMPLOYER Name: Vrana Corporation Location: Montreal Vrana Corporation is a leading biotech firm specializing in genetic engineering, advanced robotics, and military technologies. Known for secretive projects, it thrives on lucrative defense contracts and pushes the boundaries of science, often raising ethical concerns. In light of recent events, they have started a push towards funding and aiding NGOs to better their public image. ENEMY FORCES Name: Armed Takistani Locals Location: Towns and villages There are armed Takistani locals in the area of operations who are against the involvement of Contractors in the area of operations. They camouflage themselves amongs the local population and act opportunistically. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: International Development and Aid Project Location: IDAP camps The International Development and Aid Project (IDAP) are currently trying to provide the locals with food and supplies in the middle of a siege and terror of a brewing conflict. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Takistani National Army Location: Airfield The Takistani National Army has a few soldiers stationed at the local Airfield. They are neutral to contractors unless provoked. Name: Armed Takistani Locals Location: Towns and villages There are armed Takistani locals in the area of operations who may or may not be in support of Contractor presence in the area of operations. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians are present in the area, may be armed, and may or may not be in support of Contractor presence in the area of operations.
