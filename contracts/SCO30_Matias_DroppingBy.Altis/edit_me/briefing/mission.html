With the Altian civil war drawing to a close the remainder of the Old Altian Army troops have holed up in the vicinity of Oreokastro. Intelligence confirms that they are bringing in troops from Stratis to be armed at Oreokastro so that they can later start pushing out of the area in an attempt to keep fighting. As such Synixe has been hired to move into the area, and destroy the ammunition supply by any means they see fit. Contractors will be conduct a HALO insertion over the northern coast, where a NAR SOF diver team will be waiting for them with a crate containing any supplies they cannot carry on the plane. Procedure for the jump is as follows: 1- All contractors except one board the aircraft 2- The remaining contractor speaks with the local airport worker to initiate the takeoff sequence 3- The remaining contractor quickly boards the aircraft 4- As the aircraft approaches the jump location, the pilot will call "Go, go, go" Contractors will be dropping from 4500 meters. Once they achieve their objective contractors can extract to an alternate location in an old power plant.
