2nd Arganan Civil War has been waging for weeks now. With two of the rebels sides in an uneasy ceasefire, they have started pushing the government forces in rapid fashion. One member of the Arganan ballistic missile program has defected to the Sefrawi side and has offered to transfer schematics of the missiles navigation systems as well as source code to the electronic counter-countermeasures used. Unfortunately with power outages in the region he has driven closer to frontline to the town of Mantiq which has set a strict curfew and with police patroling the area, he was unable to break through. Due to our communication going through unsecured GSM network he chose to not disclose his location. His friend left a note in Rasman's Market on a "Chicken Street". Synixe has been contracted with breaking through the frontlines and secure the vital intel.
