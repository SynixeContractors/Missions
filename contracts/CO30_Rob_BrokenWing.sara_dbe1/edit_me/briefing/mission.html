The Calvane Republic has had a clandestine intelligence gathering mission operating in and around Illanz. The island nation has been flirting with joining Drovak's growing sphere of influence for some time now. Unfortunately, a MQ-47C sentinal belonging to the Calvane Intelligence Agency was shot down over Illanz. Synixe has been hired to retrieve data box aboard the drone before it can be retrieved and analyzed by Illanz forces.
