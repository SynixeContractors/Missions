After a bitter and long lasting civil war the Tanoan National government has fallen. French Legionaires have been deployed to the island to assist in evacuating civilians and loyal government forces. Synixe has been contracted by the French Foriegn Legion to aid in these efforts. Specifically, a number of high ranking government personnel have been selected for evac however, they have had to flee their positions to safe houses. Synixe is to deploy to the AO, extract them, and bring them back to Aeroport de Tanoa.
