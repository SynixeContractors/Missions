EMPLOYER Name: Légion étrangère Location: Aeroport de Tanoa France did what it could to prop up the Tanoan government during the civil war. The Legion has subsequently been deployed to Tanoa to evacuate as many civilians and loyalists as it can from the governments last secure enclave at Aeroport de Tanoa. ENEMY FORCES Name: Nouvelle République de Tanoa (NRT) Location: Most of the nation Victorious in taking the capital Georgetown, the NRT is now looking to consolidate and fully secure it's holdings over the entirety of the state. Many members, though not all, of the Tanoan Army, Navy, and Airforce defected to the NRT after the Federal government collapsed, giving the NRT control over a handful of Helicopters and APC's. Their equipment consists mainly of French small arms and light and heavy weaponry. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Gendarmerie & Army remnants Location: Safe houses While most of the Tanoan armed forces defected en-masse, the Gendarmerie mostly stayed loyal. The remaining Gendarmerie forces were responsible for getting many of the HVT's to safety after the Fenderal government collapsed. Aiding them are a number of army loyalists. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Tanoan citizens Much of the populace of Tanoa remains, unable to escape hostilities or the island.
