EMPLOYER Name: Légion étrangère Location: Aeroport de Tanoa France did what it could to prop up the Tanoan government during the civil war. Unfortunately, the government fell under relentless pressure. The Legion has subsequently been deployed to Tanoa to evacuate as many civilians and loyalists as it can ENEMY FORCES Name: Nouvelle République de Tanoa (NRT) Location: Georgetown and outlying islands Victorious in taking the capital Georgetown, the NRT is looking to consolidate and fully secure it's holdings over the entirety of the state. Many members, though not all, of the Tanoan Army, Navy, and Airforce defected to the NRT after the Federal government collapsed, giving the NRT control over a handful of Helicopters, Jet's and APC's. Their equipment consists mainly of French small arms and light and heavy weaponry. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Gendarmerie Location: Safe houses While the Tanoan armed forces defected en-masse, the Gendarmerie mostly stayed loyal. The remaining Gendarmerie forces were responsible for getting many of the HVT's to safety after the Fenderal government collapsed. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Tanoan citizens Much of the populace of Tanoa remains, unable to escape hostilities or the island.
