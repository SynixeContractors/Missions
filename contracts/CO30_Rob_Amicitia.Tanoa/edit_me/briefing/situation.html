EMPLOYER Name: Légion étrangère Location: Aeroport de Tanoa France did what it could to prop up the Tanoan government during the civil war. The Legion has subsequently been deployed to Tanoa to evacuate as many civilians and loyalists as it can from the governments last secure enclave at Aeroport de Tanoa. ENEMY FORCES Name: Nouvelle République de Tanoa (NRT) Location: Most of the nation Victorious in taking the capital Georgetown, the NRT is now looking to consolidate and fully secure it's holdings over the island. Many members, though not all, of the Tanoan Army, Navy, and Airforce defected, giving the NRT control over a small number of Helicopters and tracked vehicles. They are equipped with early 21st century weaponry, and some body armour. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Gendarmerie and Army remnants Location: Safe houses While most of the Tanoan armed forces defected en-masse, the Gendarmerie and a small contingent of the army stayed loyal. The remaining Gendarmerie forces were responsible for getting many of the HVT's to safety after the Federal government collapsed. Aiding them are a number of army loyalists. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Tanoan citizens Much of the populace of Tanoa remains, unable to escape hostilities or the island.
