Businessmen Rafael Matamoros after much success abroad is returning to invest in his homeland. As such he is constructing a new office building by the Presa Alta Dam. Eager to start he took out small loans from local businessmen for the initial phase of development. Now that he has freed up some assets he is paying off those loans. Due to an increase of local dissedents and drug related crime in the area however, he has decided that for the time being it would be safer to hire Synixe to deliver the payoff amounts. Mr. Matamoros has setup meetings with all 3 businessmen. One at the construction sight of his new office, one at the old stadium outside Avila where he played futbol as a kid, and the final one at a pier located at the workers village for the new quarry near the center of the island. While each businessman will be bringing their own private security team, Mr. Matamoros does not believe we should have any issues as Isla Pera is a remote, peaceful, quiet, jungle paradise. Thankfully Synixe has gained friends in the Ardennesse intellgence community. They have shared information with us that cartels have begun training local criminals in the art of bomb making. In addition to this they have begun to smuggle care packages of rifles of an increasing size. They warn that while townships are still safe for tourists, the jungle may be less so.
