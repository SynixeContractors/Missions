EMPLOYER Name: Tymon Stolarski Location: Germany Tymon Stolarski is a wealthy businessman with a vested interest in the political situation in Ruha. He is determined to see the release of the opposition leaders and is willing to pay handsomely for their extraction. ENEMY FORCES Name: Raven Security Location: Ruha Raven Security is a private military company with ties to the Russian government. They are well-equipped and trained, specializing in urban warfare and counter-insurgency operations. Their primary objective is to maintain control over Ruha and suppress any opposition. They have been contracted by the United Democratic Front (UDF) to secure key locations and eliminate threats to their authority. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Local Resistance Location: Ruha Some citizens of Ruha have started to organize against the UDF and Raven Security. Due to the early stages of the conflict, they are not well-equipped or organized. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Most civilians in Ruha are staying in their homes, fearing the violence that has erupted in the streets. However, some are joining the protests against the UDF and Raven Security, risking their safety for the sake of democracy.
