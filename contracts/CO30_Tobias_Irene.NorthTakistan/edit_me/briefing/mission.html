Ikwan as‑Sayf forces were recently repelled from the Al‑Munira region, but they have since regrouped and returned. IAS elements are now attempting to establish a foothold by securing Kushkak. Synixe has been contracted to support Breznian COIN operations by eliminating IAS emplacements in and around Kushkak, enabling the BAF to conduct an air‑assault and capture an IAS commander.
