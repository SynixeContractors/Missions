Recently a daring raid by Ikhwan as-Sayf on a military base allowed them to get away with the bases's weapons stash. Since the government can't spare the manpower to get back the weapons they've hired Synixe to do so. The weapons stolen consist of: - 6 cases of stolen Basic Ammo - 5 cases of stolen Basic Weapons - 4 cases of stolen Launchers The employer was smart enough to hide trackers in the boxes, so contractors know where the stolen items are.
