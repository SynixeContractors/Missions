A Khaybaran recon helicopter has gone missing while doing reconnaissance East of the Fjord area. It moved out of its AO due to weather, and last known signals show it heading southeast to northwest across Oteren. Pilots reported taking AA fire immediately before contact was lost, which is concerning as there are no currently known AA emplacements in the area. The only known recent activity in the region has been small scattered Sons of the Coming Dawn groups. Allies are concerned someone has begun supporting SotCD in the area, but have no intel to support the theory. To prevent potential political mishaps, Synixe has been hired to recover the three crewmembers (or the remains thereof). Virelia and Khaybara would also like any intel Synixe is able to gather about groups operating in, or supporting others in the region.
