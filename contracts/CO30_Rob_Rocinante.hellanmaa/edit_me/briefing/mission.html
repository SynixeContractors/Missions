Synixe has been hired by Donnager-Kwik (DK) Industries to re-take a number of their sites in the Hellanmaa region. Last month, a criminal collective overran their steel and refining plants. While Synixe was contracted immediately after the seizure by DK, our timeline for arrival has been sped up due to the collective threatening to blow up the plants should their demands not be met. DK wishes for us to reclaim their sites, defuse the explosives, and retrieve their fleet of vehicles.
