EMPLOYER Name: Donnager-Kwik Industries Donnager-Kwik has hired Synixe to retake their sites, defuse the planted explosives, and return their fleet of trucks to the FOB. ENEMY FORCES Name: Criminal Collective Location: Kouras Plant, Jankos Plant, AO Made up of bands of organized criminal elements, the group has access to small arms, AT, some CSW's, at least 6 large explosives planted at the two sites, and IED's of unknown yield. The group has made it known that they will detonate their large scale explosives between 0800 and 0830hrs should their demands not be met. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Civilians There is a scattering of civilians throughout the AO.
