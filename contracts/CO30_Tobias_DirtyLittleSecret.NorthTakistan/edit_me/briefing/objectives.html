PRIMARY - Interrogate the detainee at the Police station, northwest of Zari - Locate and neutralise Abu Tariq al-Muhandis SECONDARY - Respect the Corridor Agreement RESTRICTIONS The Corridor Agreement is a limited-access arrangement brokered by the CIA between Synixe and the Watani faction, permitting Synixe to operate within a defined zone of North Takistan. Under the terms, Synixe is authorized to move from the UN base in Mantiq to the police station northwest of Zari for the debriefing of a high-value detainee. The permitted area includes the corridor south of Mantiq, extending through and including Zari, and the Rasman valley south of Tarchi and Qorghan. All other areas—particularly south of Zari and east of Rasman—remain restricted. Watani forces have not guaranteed safe passage outside the approved zone, and any Synixe personnel spotted beyond it may be met with aggression. Strict adherence to the corridor boundaries and low-profile movement are essential to avoid escalation.
