Cartel has been expanding its operation to include international smuggling. With international pressure and internal strife and violence the government decided to contract Synixe to curtail their operations. The cartel uses light general aviation planes to move cargo off island using an improvised airfield somewhere in the jungle. A police patrol has been dispatched to try to find their positions but they have not reported back. Chancellor of the national assembly has publicized that they hired a contractor to deal with the issues, surely placing the cartel on high alert.
