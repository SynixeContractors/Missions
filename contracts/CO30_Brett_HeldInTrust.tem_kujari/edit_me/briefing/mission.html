Dr. Adam Szulc, a researcher from the Vranna corporation, working on a project in the area, has been captured by a local militia. Due to the risks of the region, his corporate insurance company, Kravtsov & Associates, has arranged for his safe extraction in exchange for a ransom payment. Synixe Contractors has been hired to carry out the extraction. The other members of the militia are unaware of the ransom agreement with some of their own members, and are still hunting for Dr. Szulc with the intention of executing him as a signal to foreign companies to stay out of the area. They will likely be hostile on sight, but may be able to be bribed. To maintain the good faith of Kravtsov & Associates, it is important that Jamal Lamrani, the militia member who found Dr. Szulc and agreed to the ransom payment, is not betrayed and the rest of the militia are not made aware of the ransom agreement.
