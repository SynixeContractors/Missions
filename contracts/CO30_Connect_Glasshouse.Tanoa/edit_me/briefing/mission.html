Three days ago, Horizon Islands Police served a warrant for an arrest for Leszek Nawrocki, a young high placed executive doing fieldwork for Integra Group in the Tanoa archipelago, for embezzlement of public funds. His mother has contracted Synixe to break out her son and clear his name by recovering his work laptop with data that would exonerate him. Leszek is scheduled to be moved from local sheriff's office to police station in Lijnhaven where he will await his trial. Monika Nawrocka has also organized his extraction out of the country. Synixe is tasked to deliver him to a jungle temple for handover. The whole island is on the edge with Volunteer Guard garrison standing by on the airfield on quick reaction force to respond to incursions by Syndikat.
