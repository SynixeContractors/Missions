Last week a Boeing 737 of Scandinavian Airlines was shot by a man portable surface-to-air missile shot from near the frontline of Finnish Civil War. Thankfully the missile fuse failed to act and the missile was stuck in the aft section of the fuselage. The plane proceeded with emergency landing in Luleå with only minor injury to one of the flight attendants that was standing near the toilet. EMPLOYER Name: Norwegian Government Norwegian government is currently preparing an armed response but they are not ready yet. They have contracted Synixe to go ahead of the army and deny heavy weapons. ENEMY FORCES Name: Sami Confederation After abolishment of Sámi Council and its cultural self-protection, group is seeking international recognition and ascension to CSAT. Armed with mostly privately acquired weapons and some Finnish service equipment seized from mobilization storage. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Iran Location: Cargo Ship Unknown threat, possibly assisting the insurgency on the ground. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians have retreated from the area
