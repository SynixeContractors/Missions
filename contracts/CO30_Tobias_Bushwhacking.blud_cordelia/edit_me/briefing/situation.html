EMPLOYER Name: Suonkomar Regional Government The Suonkomar people have been struggling for nearly a decade. Years of civil war have shown that the local government has been unable to fully quell unrest. However, they recognize the populations need for food, water, and medical aid, and have agreed to provide logistical support for IDAP to begin humanitarian operations in the southern region. They have outsourced this to Synixe. The government has supplied Synixe with the transport vehicles required for this operation and will provide pre planned artillery support, using GPS trackers in the convoy vehicles to coordinate fire missions. ENEMY FORCES Name: Sons of the Coming Dawn Location: Near Le Coq d'Or A local chapter of the Sons of the Coming Dawn has been wreaking havoc on the region for several years. Their objective is to destabilize the Suonkomar government enough to legitimize their claim for an independent and sovereign state. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Breznian Armed Forces Location: FOB Emery, Hill 114, OP Pyle, Chamkar Yok FHQ The Breznian Armed Forces have been conducting COIN operations in the region for several months, supporting the Suonkomar government in its struggle against the Sons of the Coming Dawn. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: The Suonkomar The rural population has lived under the threat of violence for years. They tend to go about their daily lives, accustomed to the instability that the civil war has brought them.
