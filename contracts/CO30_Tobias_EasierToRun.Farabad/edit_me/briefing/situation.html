EMPLOYER Name: Emirate of Al-Jatar Location: Farabad Following credible intelligence of an impending advance by the Swift Response Battalion, the Emir ordered a full evacuation of all towns and settlements east of Farabad several days ago. With no standing military of its own, the Emirate formally requested assistance from the IEA and contracted Synixe to provide immediate defensive support until coalition forces arrive. ENEMY FORCES Name: Swift Response Battalion The Swift Response Battalion (SRB) has been pushing through the region with overwhelming force, capturing settlement after settlement through massed infantry, armored vehicles, and sustained artillery fire. Recent intelligence confirms the acquisition of an Mi‑35 attack helicopter from an unidentified state actor. The SRB has also demonstrated occasional use of SVBIED attacks during previous advances, typically employed to break defensive lines or force rapid withdrawals. Expect a battalion‑sized formation numbering several hundred fighters, supported by armor, indirect fire assets, and rotary‑wing close air support. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: ION Services Location: Jazira Oil Refinery ION Services maintains on‑site security at the refinery. Due to the evacuation order, only a token force of eight volunteers remains. Prior to withdrawal, ION personnel fortified key positions and prepared several unmanned systems accessible via the terminal inside the security office. Additional weapons, ammunition, and supplies are also stored there for Synixe’s use. Name: The IEA Both Virelia and Karskoya have responded to the Emirate’s request for assistance. Virelia has deployed attack helicopters, a UCAV, and multirole fighters to the region. A Virelian UCAV is expected to be on station at approximately 10:00 and can be controlled via the terminal in the security office. Karskoya has committed an armored strike group, while Virelia’s fighters and attack helicopters are also en route. All manned air assets and the Karskoyan armored reinforcements are not expected to arrive until roughly one hour after the UCAV is on station. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Daltgreen Mining and Exploration Ltd. Location: Jazira Oil Refinery Daltgreen operates the Jazira Oil Refinery. Nearly 500 non‑essential personnel were evacuated following the Emir’s order. Remaining on‑site are 12 engineers responsible for the refinery’s multi‑day shutdown procedure, which will not be completed before the SRB is expected to arrive. The refinery also includes a small on‑site infirmary stocked with additional medical supplies that Synixe may draw upon if required. Upon arrival, Synixe is to secure and protect these engineers for the duration of the operation.
