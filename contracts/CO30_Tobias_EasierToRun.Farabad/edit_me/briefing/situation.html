EMPLOYER Name: Emirate of Al-Jatar Location: Farabad After intelligence indicated an imminent SRB advance, the Emir ordered the evacuation of all towns east of Farabad. With no standing military, the Emirate requested IEA support and contracted Synixe to provide immediate defense until coalition forces arrive. ENEMY FORCES Name: Swift Response Battalion The SRB has advanced through the region with overwhelming force, taking settlements using massed infantry, armor, and artillery. They recently acquired an Mi‑35 attack helicopter from an unknown state actor and have occasionally used SVBIEDs to break defensive lines. Expect a battalion‑sized force of several hundred fighters supported by armor, indirect fire, and rotary‑wing assets. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: ION Services Location: Jazira Oil Refinery ION Services maintains on‑site security, though only eight volunteers remain. Before withdrawing, they fortified key positions and prepared unmanned systems accessible via the security office terminal. Additional weapons, ammunition, and supplies are stored there for Synixe. Name: The IEA Virelia and Karskoya have responded to the Emirate’s request. Virelia has deployed attack helicopters, a UCAV, and multirole fighters; the UCAV will be on station at 10:00 and can be controlled from the security office. Karskoya has committed an armored strike group, and Virelia’s manned aircraft are inbound. All manned air assets and armored reinforcements are expected about one hour after the UCAV arrives. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Daltgreen Mining and Exploration Ltd. Location: Jazira Oil Refinery Daltgreen operates the refinery. Nearly 500 non‑essential staff were evacuated, leaving 12 engineers to complete the shutdown procedure, which will not finish before the SRB arrives. The site includes a small infirmary with additional medical supplies available to Synixe. Upon arrival, Synixe must secure and protect the engineers for the duration of the operation.
