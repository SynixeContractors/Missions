Synixe has been contracted by the Emirate of Al‑Jatar to reinforce the Jazira Oil Refinery following the evacuation of all settlements east of Farabad. With the Swift Response Battalion advancing rapidly through the region, the refinery’s remaining engineering staff are still completing a multi‑day shutdown procedure and cannot be withdrawn. Synixe is tasked with securing the facility, protecting the engineers, and holding the area until IEA reinforcements arrive. Intelligence indicates that the SRB vanguard intends to seize the refinery ahead of the main body’s arrival, as the site is a key strategic position for their planned siege of Farabad. The vanguard is expected to advance along the M13 highway and reach the area of operations at approximately 09:20. Additional Information: The Emir has been able to source two AH‑6 MELBs and has permitted Synixe to employ them if required.
