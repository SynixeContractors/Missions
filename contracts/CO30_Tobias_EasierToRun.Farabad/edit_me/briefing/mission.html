Synixe has been contracted by the Emirate of Al‑Jatar to reinforce the Jazira Oil Refinery after the evacuation of all settlements east of Farabad. With the Swift Response Battalion advancing rapidly, the refinery’s remaining engineers are still completing a multi‑day shutdown and cannot be withdrawn. Synixe must secure the facility, protect the engineers, and hold the area until IEA reinforcements arrive. Intelligence suggests the SRB vanguard aims to seize the refinery before the main force, as it is key to their planned siege of Farabad. The vanguard is expected to advance along the M13 highway and reach the AO at approximately 09:20. Additional Information: The Emir has sourced two AH‑6 MELBs and authorized Synixe to employ them if required.
