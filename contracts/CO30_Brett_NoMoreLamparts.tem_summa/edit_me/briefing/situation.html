EMPLOYER Name: NATO - Operation POSE (Peace Operations in Summa and Eridonia) NATO has been trying to stabilize a former Soviet dependant in Eastern Europe that has fallen into a state of civil disarray for the last 7 years. ENEMY FORCES Name: Bandits Taking advantage of the weakend state of the government, and ample supply of cold war caches, bandits have been raiding the local population and military caches. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: ION Security Location: FOB Pine A PMC hired by NATO to help stabilize the region. They have been largely ineffective in stopping the bandit raids. They are unlikely to be outside of FOB Pine. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Local Population The local population is allowed to carry rifles for the purpose of hunting. They are not to be considered hostile unless they are actively engaging friendly forces. Many carry their hunting rifles with them due to the rise in bandit activity in the area.
