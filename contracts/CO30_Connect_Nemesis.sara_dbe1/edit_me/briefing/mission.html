Over recent months the local police force has been fighting a low down rebelion on the northern island. At first it was going well, then setback after setback, loss after loss, made what was going to be quick and decisive win; 5 months of hell. With support pouring in from nearby Tiyen and constitution preventing use of armed forces, rebels have staged attacks in Bagango and Corazol. Captured mercenary has divulged information that rebels in the east are organized around 3 weapon caches. Two of them are hidden in the mountains. Wherabouts of the third one are unknown.
