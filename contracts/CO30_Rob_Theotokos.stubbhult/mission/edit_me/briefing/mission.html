Aid workers led by Priest Kajetan Sadłowski of the Virelian Orthodox church, whom were delivering aid to wartorn Gullanabba, have been captured by the Synowie nadchodzącego świtu, a communist insurrectionist group with claims on portions of Stubbhult. A demand letter has been sent to the Stubbhult government demanding a ransom along with the government recognizing the groups territorial claims. Synixe has been hired to rescue the hostages.
