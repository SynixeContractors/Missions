EMPLOYER Name: Stubbhult Republic The Stubbhult Republic has a small and underequipped military and federal police service, and has hired Synixe to retrieve the captured aid workers. ENEMY FORCES Name: Synowie nadchodzącego świtu (Sons of the Coming Dawn) (SNS) Location: AO A insurrectionist group to whom has financed their rebellion through robbery and other illicit means. They are equipped with myriad small arms, some AT weaponry, and limited body armor. They have access to some wheeled vehicle, as well as a number of CSW's. There is also the possibility that the SNS has access to mines and other explosives. It is unknown at this time where the SNS has sequestered the hostages. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Stubbhult civilians Location: Throughout the AO Most civilians have fled the AO however some still remain - unable to evacuate.
