Two days ago a huge burst of low frequency radiation was detected on a site of former radio station Mike 69 on an island of Tanna, part of Asartan Antilles. The subsequent imaging of the site shows Asartan engineers constructing a dome in the place of an old radar building and setting up makeshift base around the area. Primary objective is neutralization of the air defense network so that Khaybaran air force can conduct air strikes and bury the site. Client wants us to document the site, capture any intel and bring back any personnel involved in the operation of the site.
