EMPLOYER Name: BAF Location: Atlantic Island British forces use their post-colonial ties to the island for amphibious training. The Island has small permanent garrison used as liason and for training local troops. ENEMY FORCES Name: Third Way Party Location: Atlantic Island Local movement based on restoring precolonial identity of the Island. They have been running on a platform of removing british presence on the Island. Recently, in the north of the island, they formed an armed militia demanding government to step down. They are armed with recently stolen new and old surplus equipment from British forces including light armor vests, as well as equipment smuggled in. Symbol of rebelion is green armband. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Government Forces Location: Checkpoints Armed forces of the Republic, they are unreliable and currently kept away from direct fights. They are armed with new and old surplus equipment from United Kingdom. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Supporters of government are docile and rarely leave well populated areas amid the rebellion
