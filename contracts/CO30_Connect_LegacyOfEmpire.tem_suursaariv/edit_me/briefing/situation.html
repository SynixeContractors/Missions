EMPLOYER Name: BAF Location: Atlantic Island British forces use their post-colonial ties to the island for amphibious training. The Island has small permanent garrison used as liason and training local troops. ENEMY FORCES Name: Third Way Party Location: Atlantic Island Local movement based on restoring precolonial identity of the Island. They have been running on a platform of removing british presence on the Island. Recently, in the north of the island, they formed an armed militia demanding government to step down. They are armed with recently stolen new and old surplus equipment from British forces including light armor vests, as well as equipment smuggled in. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Government Forces Location: Checkpoints Armed forces of the Republic, they are unreliable and kept away from fights by government mandate They are armed with new and old surplus equipment from United Kingdom CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Civilians Location: Major towns Supporters of government are docile and rarely leave well populated area amid the rebellion
