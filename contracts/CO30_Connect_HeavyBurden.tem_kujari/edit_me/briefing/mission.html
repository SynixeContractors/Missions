Anti-government forces have not been contained and recently begun crossing the river. This situation critically endangers the bauxite mines in Mbokoko which significant parts of the Viatkan aviation industry rely on. Upcoming elections make direct military action unfeasible for now and they have contracted Synixe to conduct a swift strike across the river. Primary target is a mortar battery, currently believed to be stationed in the town of Danashiwa. Second point of interest is the nearby airstrip that is currently being prepared to house GA aircraft that are used for reconnaissance. Additionally Synixe is to raid the maintenance facilities set up in Kyari Kinnai where they convert civilian vehicles into armored technicals.
