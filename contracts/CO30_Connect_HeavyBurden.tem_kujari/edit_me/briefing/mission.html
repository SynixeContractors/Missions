Anti-government forces have not been contained and begun crossing the river. This situation critically endangers bauxite mines in Mbokoko which significant parts of Viatkan aviation industry relies on. Upcoming elections make direct military action not yet feasible and they have contracted Synixe to conduct a show of force across the river. Primary target is a mortar battery, currently believed to be dug in the town of Danashiwa. Second point of interest is the nearby airstrip that is currently being prepared to house GA aircraft that are used for reconnaissance. Additionally Synixe is to raid the maintaince facilities setup in Kyari Kinnai where they convert civilian vehicles into armored technicals.
