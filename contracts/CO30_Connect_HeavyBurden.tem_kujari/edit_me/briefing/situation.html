EMPLOYER Name: Viatka Viatkan government is very concerned with the security of bauxite mining operations in the north of Kujari region. Unable to mobilize support for direct military action, they contracted Synixe to conduct a show of force against the rebellion. ENEMY FORCES Name: Swift Response Battalions Popular movement leading a civil war in Mbokoko. They are equipped with very varied equipment leading pushes with technicals and terror bombings with mortars. Many of the counteroffensives by the government have encountered anti-tank mines on approach and got ambushed near the crossings. With government troops abandoning their positions along the river, they are pushing north have gained ground in the east. Lead by a ruthless former general of Mbokokan army, Hugo Molina. Name: Groupe Valmont Hired as advisors by an unknown source, they help with operation of specialized equipment and act as personal detail of regional commanders. They also serve as forward observers and call in mortar fire missions. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Mbokokan Gendarmerie Location: North of the river Beaten back from the river crossing they have retreated to the north. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians are fleeing the war.
