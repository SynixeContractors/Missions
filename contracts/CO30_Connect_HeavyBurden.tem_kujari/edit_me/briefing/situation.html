EMPLOYER Name: Viatka Viatkan government is very concerned with the security of bauxite mining operations in the north of Kujari region. Unable to mobilize support for direct military action, they contracted Synixe to conduct a show of force against the rebellion. ENEMY FORCES Name: Solo Forza A popular movement leading a civil war in Mbokoko. They are equipped with varied stolen gear, leading pushes with technicals and terror bombings with mortars. Many of the government's counteroffensives have encountered anti-tank mines on approach and were ambushed near the crossings. With government troops abandoning their positions along the river, the rebels are pushing north and have gained ground in the east. They are led by Hugo Molina, a ruthless former general of the Mbokokan army. Name: Groupe Valmont Hired as advisors by an unknown source, they help with operation of specialized equipment and act as personal detail of regional commanders. They also serve as forward observers and call in mortar fire missions. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Mbokokan Gendarmerie Location: North of the river Beaten back from the river crossing they have retreated to the north. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians are fleeing the war.
