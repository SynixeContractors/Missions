EMPLOYER Name: PrimeFlux Energy Location: Orlec Factory PrimeFlux Energy is a multinational energy conglomerate operating throughout the region. Unofficially, the company serves as a commercial front for the Virelian Intelligence Agency, providing legal cover and logistical access in areas where direct state involvement would be politically sensitive. Synixe has been contracted under this commercial pretext to provide security for PrimeFlux’s facility in Drakovac. The cover will be blown if contractors are caught south of the Breznian security border. ENEMY FORCES Name: Sons of the Coming Dawn Location: Southern part of the peninsula The Sons of the Coming Dawn have complete control of the peninsula south of the Breznian security border, operating openly in towns, villages, and rural terrain. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Synixe Contractors - Charlie Team Location: Near Hrasta Contractors from Charlie Team have secured an extraction point at the port near Hrasta. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Republic of Breznia Location: Northern part of the peninsula and western islands. The Breznian Armed Forces have been conducting counterinsurgency operations in Drakovac for several months, attempting to reassert control over the southern peninsula and the surrounding islands. Their presence is strongest in the north and along key transit routes. Breznian forces are expected to initiate their own recovery operation for the downed helicopter and its occupants. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Drakovac Local Administration The civilian population of Drakovac is dispersed across small coastal towns and inland villages. While officially under Breznian governance, local allegiances vary, with some communities sympathetic to the Sons of the Coming Dawn and others cooperating with government forces.
