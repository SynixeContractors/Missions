As part of COIN operations in Drakovac, Breznian Special Forces conducted a raid on the island of Lubenice with the objective of extracting a foreign asset codenamed “The Prince.” Virelian intelligence has since confirmed the asset’s identity as Mikhael Weiss. During the extraction phase, the helicopter transporting the asset went down for unknown reasons somewhere north to northwest of Zatvorski Iogor. Synixe has been tasked with locating and recovering the asset, along with any other survivors, before Breznian forces can mount their own recovery effort and before hostile insurgent elements can find them. Additional information: Virelian intelligence recently intercepted Breznian internal communications regarding the incident: 02:38 - Extraction under fire reported 02:42 - Anomaly in controls detected 02:44 - Loss of control reported 02:47 - Mayday transmitted 02:49 - Final contact lost Standard Breznian aircrew survival protocol instructs downed personnel to intermittently activate their transponder and relocate between transmissions to reduce the risk of detection by hostile forces. Using the intercepted data, Virelian intelligence has identified the frequency being used. The surviving aircrew have been transmitting approximately every 20 minutes, providing a narrow but actionable window for Synixe to track their movements. In response to the incident, Breznian air assets have been temporarily grounded until Breznian command can confirm the aircraft was not brought down by hostile anti‑air systems.
