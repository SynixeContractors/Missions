EMPLOYER Name: Central Intelligence Agency, US Department Of Defense ENEMY FORCES Name: Takistani Army Location: Airfield, Villa, Zargabad Their equipment varies between ranks, conscripted troops have been seen to carry cold war era weaponry along with old fatigues and chest rigs. Their more specialized troops however carry modern weapons along with uniforms and harnesses provided by CSAT. They also posses light vehicles along with some emplacements and the odd APC or IFV. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians are present in the city of Zargabad as well as the surrounding areas.
