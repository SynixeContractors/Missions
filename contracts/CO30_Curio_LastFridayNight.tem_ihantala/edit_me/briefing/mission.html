Last week Oskar Mazur, a local politician, was attending a nightclub outside of the region. There, he was caught on video discussing the people he serves in an extremely negative light, calling them the likes of "disgusting" and "freeloaders" which ultimately ended up in local outrage in the already unstable region. Mazur's big push for agricultural outreach and progress to stave off famine makes him a valuable asset to the Integrated Economic Alliance, specifically to Virelia. Whilst the IEA and Virelia detest the comments made by Mazur, they wish for him to be transported safely out of the region. The only lead given to Synixe from Virelia is Mazur's big hobby in offroading. The last contact with Mazur was with his mechanic Davren, whose automobile repair center is located west of Hakamäki.
