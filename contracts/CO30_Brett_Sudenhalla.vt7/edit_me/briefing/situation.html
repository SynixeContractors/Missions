EMPLOYER Name: Virojoki Government Location: Virojoki The Virojoki Government is a local authority struggling to maintain control over the region. They only have standard law enforcement, which are not numerous enough to deal with the rapid moving bandits. ENEMY FORCES Name: Bandit Group Location: Marked Area, North West of Virojoki Starting as a "defensive" group to teach young men survival skills, they have become a radicalized and violent group. Equipped with last gen Russian weapons, they are well armed and quick to move around the forest.
