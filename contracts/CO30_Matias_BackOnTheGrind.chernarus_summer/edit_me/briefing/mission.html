Recently a small scale civil conflict has erupted in Chernarus, with a group of government dissidents comprised by local disenfranchised young men wreaking havoc on the Chernarussian countryside. With supplies and services being cut off to the towns in the region, IDAP has established several relief points throught the countryside. Since they are worried that these men will open fire on aid workers while they deliver supplies, they've opted to hire Synixe for the deliveries. Contractors must deliver the required supplies to the different IDAP camps and centres while adhereing to the restrctions stipulated. There is also an IDAP train outbound from the Elektrzavodsk Railyard meant to deliver supplies to which contractors must deliver supplies. Contractors failing to adhere to these restrctions could result in the contract being cancelled. Delivery list: - Elektrzavodsk Railyard: Equipment kit (Supply box, Exposives box and Uniforms Box), truckload of food, 8x box of medical supplies - Kozlovka: 2x Heap of grain, 2x Heap of rice, 2x box of medical supplies - Putoshka: 4x box of medical supplies - Stary Sobor: Equipment kit (Supply box, Exposives box and Uniforms Box), 4x box of medical supplies Contractors have been provided with IDAP trucks for easier delivery of the supplies, with the truck bound for the railyard having already been loaded.
