A VIS asset, Fadey Zharkov, has been captured by members of the Luban Liberation Front. Synixe has been tasked with retrieving him and safely delivering him to the airfield designated Hotel. EMPLOYER Name: Virelian Intelligence Service (VIS) The VIS has provided intel on Zharkov's last known location, a compound designated Quebec, as well as the Luban Liberation Front. They have arranged insertion via Ghost Hawk, as well as a flight out of the country for Zharkov originating at the airfield designated Hotel. Thay believe Zharkov to be alive, but is concerned he will be non-ambulatory. They have also relayed intel that several vehicles have been moved by what they believe to be friendly assets to the area designated VRP Bravo. Zharkov was their largest asset in the region, so they have been unable to completely validate this intel. ENEMY FORCES Name: Luban Liberation Front Location: Spread across the main peninsula. A self-proclaimed liberation force that has gained considerable influence due to recent destabilization in the region. Intel reports they are well-equipped and are known to have roving patrols that sometimes include technicals. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Civilians Location: Entire Region Civilian consideration should be paramount, as the region is already spiraling into instability.
