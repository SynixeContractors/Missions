EMPLOYER Name: NATO With the VPR distracted in Virojoki, an opportunity has arisen for NATO to procure valuable intelligence on the Y-32 to which the VPR has acquired. They also wish to supply the KV with the three weapon shipments the VPR has recently received. ENEMY FORCES Name: Virolahati Peoples Republic (VPR) and CSAT Armed forces Location: Virojoki and nearby airfield The VPR is currently undertaking a large COIN operation in and around the city of Virokoki. As this is occurring, the VPR is accepting the handover of a Y-32 and a large weapons shipment from CSAT forces at the nearby airfield. VPR forces are equipped with small arms, AT weapons, a number of wheeled assets, and a SAM site at the airfield. The small CSAT force is well equipped with modern weaponry and armor. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Kansan Vapausliike (KV) Location: Around Virojoki and Harajanniemi Airfield The KV was created by NATO to counter the growing ambitions of the VPR. They are lightly armed and armoured. Local forces will be attempting to distract VPR forces while Synixe undertakes their operation. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Civilians Location: Virokoki and surrounding areas The civilian populace of the VPR is present within the AO.
