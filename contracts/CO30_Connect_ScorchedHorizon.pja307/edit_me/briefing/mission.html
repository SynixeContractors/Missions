Last month's election results have been internationally disputed due to the widespread use of the Luban Army to guard the polling places, leading to severe voter intimidation. The government has said those measures had to be taken due to the terror threat caused by supporters of the Luban Liberation Front. With the vote blocked in UN Security Council, Mbakoran Army moved in a thunder run into the capital without firing a shot and oversaw the swearing-in of provisional LLF Government. The Military, in disarray has mostly joined the new government, with small pockets of resistance. The Khaybaran government, a supporter of the previous regime has vowed to restore peace in Luba. Ahead of the invasion Khaybaran special forces have infiltrated through the frontline to target enemy C2. With one of the teams missing in the Dariyah region, Synixe was contracted to fufil their mission of destroying the local cellular network ahead of attack. The invasion will begin at H-Hour with a missile barage and subsequent mechanized assault into Ben Guerrir. After acomplishing their objectives Synixe is to support assault into Ben Guerrir. H-Hour has been set for 07:20 local time.
