The IEA has become increasingly worried that Altis's weapons research and development program has become too advanced. Intelligence suggests they're developing superior techonology in several fields including helicopters, handheld weaponry and experimental weaponry. Therefore they have hired Synixe to mobilize into Stratis, which has been used as the base for the weapons program for the last 20 years, and secure the scientists mainly associated with the program. Contractors must secure the scientists in charge of the helicopter research in Camp Rogain, handheld weapons research in the Kamino Firing Range, and the scientists in charge of something under the codename Alpha-Omega-3 which is being developed in the Stratis Airbase. Below is the amount of scientists assigned to each program: - Helicopter research: 2 scientists - Handheld weapons research: 3 scientists - Alpha-Omega-3: 4 scientists
