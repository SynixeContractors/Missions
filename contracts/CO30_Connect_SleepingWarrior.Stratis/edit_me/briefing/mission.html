After a swift campaign the island of Haranis has been conquered by Illianzian forces. Over the past weeks they have hunted down local resistance from the former garrison and civilians who picked up arms. Illanzian tactics consist of a rapid and overwhelming response to threats and they have managed to decimate friendly rebel forces in very short time. Their forces now are much more spread out on the island and the time to strike is now. Synixe has been contracted to destroy anti-air facilities at Mike-26. The air station also contains significant logistical and intelligence importance to Illanzian forces on the island and previous attempts by FIA have been crushed by wide mobilization of forces in the nearby areas.
