EMPLOYER Name: Nyrland The occupation of Island of Haranis by Illianzan forces is unacceptable to the government of Nyrland. Originally supporting the local resistance, with the Illanzian heavy use of fast reaction forces to quell any local actions they have turned to unconventional means and hired Synixe to disrupt the occupier. ENEMY FORCES Name: Illanzian Armed Forces Decently well armed, and very well organized force, known for their prowess in COIN operation by using very flexible and reaction force oriented tactics that have decimated FIA operations. They currently have a transport helicopter and wheeled tank at their disposal. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: FIA Location: Hiding in forests Decimated by actions of Illanz they have gone into hiding. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilian live goes on as normal.
