The Calvane Intelligence Service has hired Synixe to extract an operative. Undercover operative John Smith has supposedly been arrested for a traffic incident in Drovak. Before his real identity is discovered he needs to be extracted. Contractors will be transported to the Drovak coast under cover of night aboard the CS Artful. Once ashore contractors will have to find their own land transportation. Contractors may be able to acquire civilian vehicles in the nearby coastal towns of Nizhnoye and/or Solnichniy. Once transportation is acquired, Calvane intelligence believes that their Agent Smith may have been arrested by police from either Dolina or Mogilevka and may be being held in one of their jails. Calvane intelligence stresses that we are to leave no trace of our being here. If contractors are engaged they are to leave no witnesses that can identify them. While brief skirmishes with local police may go unnoticed, prolonged encounters are far more likely to cause issues. Contractors are advised to avoid the naval port in Electrozavodsk. If the Drovak military is alerted to the contractors presence they will most likely respond with air support from their nearby carrier group. Contractors are advised to not be followed back to the rendezvous. If a tail is detected, the captain of the Artful has orders to submerge and leave the contractors behind in order to remain undetected.
