EMPLOYER Name: Emirate of Al Jatar Location: Farabad The Emirate of Al Jatar has no standing military, relying instead on foreign private security contractors and local police for national defense. Despite this, it plays an active role in regional security through intelligence sharing and the targeted employment of contractors to enforce its security needs. ENEMY FORCES Name: Swift Response Battalion Location: Al Salman Outskirts The SRB has advanced through the region with overwhelming force, capturing settlements using massed infantry, armed vehicles, and artillery. They are currently entrenched around the outskirts of Al Salman, operating three artillery batteries that are continuously bombarding the city. The SRB lacks dedicated air defense, and heavy armor. However, contractors should still expect a well armed militia force capable of coordinated resistance. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Synixe - Charlie Team Location: Nugrat Al Salman Contractors from Charlie Team have secured an extraction point at the Nugrat Al Salman. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Security Forces and Local Militia Location: Al Salman Following the disbandment of Al Salman’s military two years ago, veteran fighters and police officers have formed an all volunteer Security Force, supported by a civilian militia. They are lightly armed, poorly equipped. Communication with the defenders has not been established. Due to this and their lack of nightvision equipment, they are likely to engage any armed personnel on sight. Contractors are advised to avoid Al Salman to prevent Green on Blue incidents. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Al Salman The outskirts of Al Salman are sparsely inhabited. Most civilians have evacuated the area.
