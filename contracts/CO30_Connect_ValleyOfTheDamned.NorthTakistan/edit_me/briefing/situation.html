EMPLOYER Name: Agence France-Presse International news agency headquartered in Paris. Their war correspondent and his cameraman have gone missing after being separated from government forces during recent offensive. ENEMY FORCES Name: Northern Alliance Post-Mujahideen faction strongly opposed to Taliban-led Kabul Government mostly composed of ethnicities from northern Afghanistan such as Tajiks and Uzbeks. Mostly a militia force, with small arms and technicals with few captured MRAPs. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: ION Location: Marked outposts Private security for international companies interested in working with Taliban government. They are currently formulating exit plans from the area. By agreement with AFP they will provide us access to their compound's technical resources. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan Location: Mantiq Government forces are poorly trained and have pulled back from the area. We have the required permissions from the government but we don't know what happens if the push comes to shove. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians present throughout the area.
