One of the new plantations of the Jaya Group has been torched by one of the groups of Free Papua Movement, calling for the end of the extractive economy of the region. The plantation was briefly shelled from across the river, the suspected areas are marked in blue. Rebel captured by company's private security has divulged information that the rebels utilize the river and the nearby areas to transport equipment and personel around the area, using old trails and fishing piers. From the rebel's testimony they are 3 groups, camping only few hundred meters in land but they move their camps every few days, last known position of one is near the long island. Synixe has been contracted to find the mortars and destroy the camps.
