Early in the morning site 7-B belonging to the Fjord Intelligence Agency was attacked by Groupe Valmont with the intent of obtaining several sensitive assets. The assets consist of communications equipment along with servers that were prepped for transport in an Astra van, a device currently under R and D and three chemical containers. Before Groupe Valmont could fully take over the facility a fireteam made it out of the site with the device in the back of a HEMTT truck. They're currently waiting in Port Nervei for an extraction. For the time being Groupe Valmont has been contained to the Norrfjorden bay, as a roadblock was quickly established south of Igeldas to prevent them from heading South and the team at the Nervei Port has not seen any movement headed West.
