Illanz forces have created and are moving experimental tech to an airfield under their control in the area. Virelia and it's allies want to know what this tech is and it's capabilities before it is used against them. The convoy moving this tech encountered vehicle trouble several kilometers away from the airfield north of the town of Grishino. Synixe has been contracted to steal, and repair if necessary, the two HEMTT container vehicles moving with the convoy and return them to the safehouse. Contractors should keep in mind the strong Illanz presence in the area, and expect swift and trained QRF forces.
