EMPLOYER Name: Aliabad Regional Police Location: Scattered across south-western villages. An undercover operator has actionable intelligence suggesting that the Coltan Mine is being used as a cover for a lucrative black market storage site. As the police have been driven back by local freedom fighters, they have hired Synixe as a last resort. ENEMY FORCES Name: Ikhwan as-Sayf Location: Defending Wolak and surrounding areas. Local freedom fighters who have persistently attacked police forces. After taking Wolak, Ikhwan as-Sayf has taken control of the Coltan Mine and is defending the surrounding area, using it as leverage against the government. Wolak is heavily defended as it is the sole entrance to the Coltan Mine. Name: The Professionals Location: Operating a lucrative black market out of the Coltan Mine This group of professionals was hired to assist Ikhwan as-Sayf as "private security." Intelligence indicates that Ikhwan as-Sayf and The Professionals have struck a deal: The Professionals provide money and weapons, and in return Ikhwan as-Sayf stays quiet about their location and motives. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Aliabad Regional Police Location: Scattered across south-western villages. The police have set up patrols in Dawlatsah, Ahmad Golam, Darshag, Narmaki, Hadiz, and Abad. There are two checkpoints, one located in Hadiz on the MSR, and one south of Ahmad Golam. Forces from Wolak and Katal have been pushed back into Abad, acting to defend the village. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Due to the recent and ongoing violence, all civilians have fled the area.
