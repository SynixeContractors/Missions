EMPLOYER Name: Aliabad Regional Police Location: Scattered across south-western villages. An undercover operator has actionable intelligence suggesting that the Coltan Mine has been being used as a cover for a lucrative black market storage center. As the police have been driven back due to local freedom fighters, they have hired Synixe as a last resort. ENEMY FORCES Name: Ikhwan as-Sayf Location: Defending Wolak and surrounding areas. Local freedom fighters who have persistently attacked police forces. After taking Wolak, IaS has since taken control of Coltan Mine and defended the surrounding area, using it as leverage against the government. Wolak is heavily defended as it is the sole enterance of Coltan Mine. Name: The Professionals Location: Operating a lucrative black market out of the Coltan Mine This group of professionals were hired to assist the IaS as "private security." It had been found out the IaS and the Professionals have stuck a deal. The Professionals give gifts to the IaS in forms of money and weaponry, and in turn, the IaS stays quiet about their location and motives. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Aliabad Region Police Location: Scattered across south-western villages. The police have set up patrols in Dawlatsah, Ahmad Golam, Darshag, Narmaki, Hadiz, and Abad. There are two checkpoints, one located in Hadiz on the MSR, and one south of Ahmad Golam. Forces from Wolak and Katal have been pushed back into Abad, acting to defend the village. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Due to the recent and ongoing violence, all civilians have fled the area.
