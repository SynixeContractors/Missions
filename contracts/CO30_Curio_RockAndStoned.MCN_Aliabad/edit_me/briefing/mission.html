An undercover Aliabad Regional Police operator reports that the Coltan Mine is being used as a cover for a lucrative black market storage site. As police forces have been driven back by local freedom fighters, they have hired Synixe as a last resort. Ikhwan as-Sayf has hired a group of professionals as "private security" for the mine after assaulting and securing Wolak. Coltan Mine was raided by an Aliabad Regional Police SWAT team who failed to make entry to the mine. The same insider has given Aliabad Regional Police details of the convoy. The target vehicle is a cargo LUT carrying multiple boxes. Also in the convoy is an armed technical and a transport LUT carrying the remaining forces out of the mine, including the insider "Mohammad Islami" who is to be recovered and not harmed. The convoy will step off around 06:15 local time. It will head south from the mine, turning east from Wolak. It will continue through Hadiz, then south through Narmaki, continuing south until they have left the region.
