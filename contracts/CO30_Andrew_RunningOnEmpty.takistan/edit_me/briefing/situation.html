The oil fields of Takistan are under threat from the Taliban. With a ION PMC unit rogue and off the grid, Synixe is hired to respond and assist. EMPLOYER Takistani Government The Takistani Government has been dealing with Taliban forces for decades. A constant thorn in the side of the government, these terrorist forces are focused on ruining the oil infastructure to disrupt drilling. ENEMY FORCES The Taliban The Taliban are well known, often using force to spread their ideology and beliefs. There are reports of them being equipped with small arms, RPGs, vehicles, and some statics weaponry. FRIENDLY FORCES ION PMC Location(s): FOB at Lalezar, Par-e Siah oilfield, other oil fields in the region. ION PMC has been contracted to act as security for numerous oilfields. With one ION PMC group going radio silent, we are called to help and investigate with their numbers spread thin. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians are present in the area and may or may not be in support of either Synixe Contractors, ION PMC, or the Taliban.
