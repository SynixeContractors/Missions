We have been contracted to prevent attacks on critical infrastructure that may occur during the annual Mayson Festival. The environmental protesters have not been known to be violent, and we believe that the majority of the protesters will not be violent. However, we have received intelligence that a small group is planning to cause damage to industrial sites, bridges in the area, and most dangerously, the dam. The city officials have decided to continue with the festival, and want to avoid further upsetting their citizens. They have asked us to keep a low profile, and to avoid using lethal force unless absolutely necessary.
