For the last three months the Sons of the Coming Dawn milita movement has been gathering strength in the Korsac region of Lybor. In the beginning the KDF did not percieve them as a threat and since they're short staffed and lacking resources they decided to leave them be for the time being. That all changed 2 weeks ago when an Interpol wiretap on international arms dealer Michael Jones confirmed that a weapons shipment was due to arrive at the SotCD stronghold in the town of Kinney, but by the time the KDF was able to set up an operation to stop the SotCD it was already to late and the shipment had already arrived. Now the KDF is hiring Synixe to mobilize into the region and crush the SotCD by destroying their newfound supplies. Additionally we're being asked by Interpol to track down and arrest Michael Jones, the wiretap confirms that he is still in the region and that the last call made from his personal cellphone was made at 15:51. Unfortunately Jones turned of his phone too quickly and as a result his exact location could not be obtained.
