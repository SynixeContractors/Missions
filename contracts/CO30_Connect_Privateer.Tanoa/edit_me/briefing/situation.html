EMPLOYER Name: NeverBlue Marine Corporation A maritime shipping company, focused on container movement across the ocean. One of their ships recently fell victim to a pirate group operating out of Gorgados Islands and the company had to pay the ransom for ship and the crew. After unsuccessful talks with the island's authority they decided to contract Synixe to break down the criminal gangs engaging in piracy in the nearby sea. ENEMY FORCES Name: Unknown pirate groups Location: Southwestern islands There is no information on the group's composition. The security footage from MV Nevertaken shows armed men approaching on fast boats with machineguns firing across the deck. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Gorgados Police Underfunded, corrupt and apathetic to the pirate groups as long as they don't cause trouble on the island. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians continue to live their normal lives.
