EMPLOYER Name: Cambodian Federal Government The Cambodian government has been struggling to contain the RRB for years now. A number of units Cambodian army and air units have forayed into the AO however, they have not been heard from. ENEMY FORCES Name: Ronsersa Ruobruom Brachachon (RRB) & Unknown Chinese PMC Location: Temples and elsewhere within the AO The RRB is a communist insurgency group that has expanded in recent years thanks to China and other CSAT alligned nations taking an interest in bringing Cambodia into their sphere of influence. RRB forces are limited to mostly 20th century weaponry however, they have acquired the services of an unknown Chinese PMC. It is presumed this PMC has access to modern weaponry and vehicles. Intelligence reports that the stolen artifacts have been relocated to a centralized location within the AO. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Cambodian citizens Location: Throughout the AO The RRB has not taken advantage of the citizens within the AO thus, there has been no exodus of the citizenry.
