The Ronsersa Ruobruom Brachachon (RRB), a Cambodian insurgency group, has been waging an incesent guerilla war against the Federal government for over a decade now. Recently, they have taken to looting the countries ancient temples to fuel their war. The Cambodian government has hired Synixe to relcaim the stolen artifacts, take back the temples, and free any hostages the RRB has taken.
