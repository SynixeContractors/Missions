The invasion of Malden by Altis Armed Forces was predicted after long diplomatic buildup and movement of amphibious ready group of Altian Navy to the other side of Mediterranean Sea. What was not predicted, is the extreme success MDF has had in usage of drones to attack landing zones and Maldeneese Integrated Air Defense System holding air superiority over the northern side of the island. This prevents AAF from using its fast air assets and led to deployment of many artillery systems by invading force. The government of Malden has brought onboard many PMC groups to train Malden Defense Force in assymetric warfare. Synixe task was to stay in hiding and prepare an attack onto the rear area of Altian forces.
