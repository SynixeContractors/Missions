At midnight, a mission was launched to recover two undercover Karskoyan Intelligence Agency operatives from the airfield base on Utes. Unfortunately, both helicopters were shot down before they could land. As a result, Synixe has been contracted to deploy to the island, extract the operatives, and neutralize the Livonian Defense Force's anti-aircraft capabilities. The operatives consist of one maintenance worker wearing an orange vest and one laborer wearing a yellow hard hat. Both are carrying weapons to maintain their cover but will not engage contractors. Additionally, Synixe is requested to rescue any surviving members of the two downed squads and recover the bodies of those killed, including the helicopter pilots where possible. We know that at least one pilot survived, as he was the individual who radioed in the report that the helicopters had been shot down. His last transmission indicated that he was hiding at a pier on the northern coast of the island.
