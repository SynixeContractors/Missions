At midnight a mission was launched to recover two undercover Karskoyan intelligence agency operatives from the airfield base in Utes. Unfortunately both helicopters were shot down before being able to land. Therefore Synixe has been hired to move to the island and extract the operatives as well as neutralise the LDF's AA capabilites. The operatives consist of one maintainer wearing and orange vest and one worker wearing a yellow hard hat. They both will carry weapons to maintain appereances but will not shoot at contractors. Additionally they would like contractors to if possible rescue and/or recover the bodies of the two squads and their pilots. We know at least one of the pilots survived because he is the one that radio'd in that they were shot down, he last reported he is hiding in a pier in the north of the island.
