Recently a convoy transporting weapons belonging to the Korsac Defense Legion (KDF) was attacked near the town of Zerno. Synixe has been hired to investigate the location of the attack and find out where the weapons have been taken. Once found the weapons are to be recovered and brought back to the contractor's FOB. The transport was carrying: - 4 crates of explosives - 1 crate of basic weapons - 1 crate of special weapons - 1 crate of launchers - 1 crate of grenades - 1 crate of basic ammo
