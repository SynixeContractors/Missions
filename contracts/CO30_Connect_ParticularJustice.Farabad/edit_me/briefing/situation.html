EMPLOYER Name: Norox Location: Mines in the northern area Joint venture between two European mining conglomerates, focused on resource extraction in Uzbekistan. Their mines have recently come under more and more pressure from Hizb ut-Tahrir group militias coming from the border region of Afghanistan. ENEMY FORCES Name: Hizb ut-Tahrir After years of low activity the central Asian parts of the group have become more active. Recruiting from shunned minority groups, partially supported by the Taliban government, they've been able to acquire weapons left behind from American occupation of Afghanistan as well as smuggled in equipment from China. Local group wants to push government to abolish its secular constitution and join federation of similar movements across the region. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: ION Location: Mine Security Small in numbers, provide local security to mine workers. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians present in the area
