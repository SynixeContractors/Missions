EMPLOYER Name: Freedom and Independence Army (FIA) and Virelia The FIA has requested assistance from Virelia to halt the Dal Riadan advance. As Virelia is unable to deploy troops directly, it has provided funding to enable the FIA to hire PMCs in support of their operations. ENEMY FORCES Name: Dal Riada Location: Airbases in the southern part of the region A well-equipped military force employing air assets and ground vehicles to support its ongoing advance and occupation of the region. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Freedom and Independence Army (FIA) Location: FOB A local militia currently resisting the Dal Riadan advance. One squad remains at the FOB to provide security while Synixe conducts the operation. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: IDAP Location: Gullanabba and Yaber IDAP has established an outpost and is conducting unexploded ordnance (UXO) clearance operations while providing humanitarian assistance to civilians in the affected towns. Additional civilians may be present throughout the region.
