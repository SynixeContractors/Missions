For the past several months, Dal Riada has been steadily advancing into the region, gradually occupying all territory in its path. As is common during an invasion, a local militia was formed and has been conducting operations against the occupying forces. Recently, Dal Riada's advance has stalled in this region. In an apparent attempt to demoralize the civilian population, they have begun conducting air strikes against civilian settlements. Two days ago, they bombed the central towns of Gullanabba and Yaber, crippling rail lines and port facilities. Unable to effectively counter these air attacks, the FIA requested assistance from Virelia. Virelia has provided funding, allowing the FIA to hire private military contractors to reinforce their capabilities. Synixe has been contracted to assault Dal Riada's airbases in the region and destroy their air assets. Additionally, Synixe is authorized to destroy any other Dal Riada military equipment, infrastructure, or assets encountered during the operation. To facilitate the assault on the southernmost airbase, the FIA has concealed several dinghies near the church on the shores of Lake Nejsjön.
