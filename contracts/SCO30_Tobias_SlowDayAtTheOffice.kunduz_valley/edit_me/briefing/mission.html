A Virelian diplomat was en route to visit the Grand Mosque of Maghdoot Khel when his convoy was attacked by members of the Swift Response Battalion. This is peculiar because Maghdoot Khel is Ikhwan as‑Sayf territory. Virelian Intelligence has informed us that the IAS and SRB forces have been fighting over the region. ISR has revealed that the HVI has been moved to a building near the ambush site, and IAS forces are amassing to the south of the city
