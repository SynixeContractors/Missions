With the constant advance of Khaybaran forces deeper into Luba, manpower shortages have persisted despite integrating government forces into their chain of command. The Luban Liberation Front has crossed the river and cut off many supply lines supporting the forces holding the flanks. Synixe has been contracted to retake the river crossings and deliver supplies to a mechanized platoon stranded on the other side of the river. The Luban Liberation Front has been known to mine approaches to various fords with anti-tank mines.
