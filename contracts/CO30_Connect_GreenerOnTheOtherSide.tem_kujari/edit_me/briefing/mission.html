With the constant advanced of Khaybaran forces deeper into Luba, their manpower shortages, though helped by integrating the government forces directly in their chain of command, have continued. Luban Liberation Front has crossed the river and cut off many supply lines into the forces holding the flanks. Synixe has been contracted to retake the river crossings and deliver supplies to mechanized platoon stuck on the other side of the river. LLF has been known to mine approaches to various fords with anti tank mines.
