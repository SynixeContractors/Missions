Altis has been struck by an 8.1 magnitude earthquake, aftershocks have been making the region extremely hazardous. Search and Rescue (SAR) operations were temporarily halted when looters threatened and engaged rescue workers. It resulted in several injured, that are now being treated at Kavala General hospital. Synixe has been hired to resume SAR operations; finding and extracting casualties, marking the deceased for recovery and securing the towns from looters and other criminals.
