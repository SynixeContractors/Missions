The Chernarus Police have received a tip from inside a separatists movement about an plan to attack 6 targets throughout the region. A chemical shipment was brought into the port of Elektrozavodsk, and was moved to an unknown location to create bombs. The CI (Mikhail Mikhaylova) doesn't know the bomb factory or the targets, but is involved at a secondary location used as an armoury and safehouse. We are being provided vehicles and a chemical detection equipment to help us locate the bombs.
