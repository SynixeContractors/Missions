EMPLOYER Name: Columbian Government Location: Nation Wide Given the untimely deadline given to it by the rebels, and issues in supplying it's own forces, the federal government has reached out to Synixe to assist in revolving this matter. ENEMY FORCES Name: Fuerzas Armadas Revolucionarias Socialistas Location: Presa Alta, and surrounding areas An armed socialist revolutionary group which has a considerable supply of weaponry and manpower at it's disposal. They are equipped with 90's - mid 2000's small arms, some light-med CSW's, and possibly light APC's. It is believed they have planted at least 4 explosives FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Columbian Army and State Police Location: Outside the AO Both the army and state police lack the manpower and assets to combat the rebels. All of their attempts at dislodging them have so far failed. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Columbian civilians Many civilians are still present throughout the AO, though a good number were able to evac prior to the take-over.
