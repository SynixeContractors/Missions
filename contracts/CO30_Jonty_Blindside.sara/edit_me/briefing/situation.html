The northern region of Sahrani Island is under NATO control, while the southern region is dominated by the Liberation Army. Last night, a NATO barracks was attacked by unidentified assailants. All personnel managed to evacuate, except for two sergeants who were captured while defending the transport trucks. Intelligence reports indicate that the Liberation Army infiltrated the northern island via an unguarded coastline and established a foothold in the abandoned camps on the hilltops of the Sierra Pita Valley. EMPLOYER Name: NATO Location: Northern Sahrani Island ENEMY FORCES Name: Liberation Army Location: Southern Sahrani Island, Sierra Pita Valley The Liberation Army is a disciplined militia with a history of employing guerrilla warfare tactics. They are armed with military-grade weaponry, and possess extensive knowledge of the local terrain, which they leverage to their advantage. Their objectives are to disrupt NATO operations and seize control of the northern region. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Sahrani Civilians Location: Throughout Sahrani Island
