Sons of the Coming Dawn have used recent events as a catalyst for unrest in Farabad. They have rallied disenfranchised locals into taking up arms and rioting, securing some southern entrances to the city and pushing northward. Local Gendarmerie forces were overrun, but are attempting a large push to quell the violence. A Tempest Research executive (Joe Harris) and two of their most valued scientists (Ahmad Alavi and Siavash Zare) were in a meeting when the unrest escalated, and are currently taking shelter in the Tempest Research Facility in Farabad. Tempest Research wants them extracted back to the airfield safely.
