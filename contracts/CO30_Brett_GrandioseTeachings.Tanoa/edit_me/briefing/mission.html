A group calling themselves "The True Sons", under the leadership of Pierre Ngunga, have taken control Lijnhaven and surrounding villages. For the past several years, they have been recruiting Tanoan diaspora to their cause, promising a better, traditional, deserved future on their islands. The True Sons follow the teachings of their leader, Ngunga, who has been preaching to them that they have a duty to reclaim their homeland from the globalist sellouts currently in power. They have acquired a significant amount of weaponry and resources, and have undergone training to prepare for this attack. 2 hours ago, a C-130 landed at Saint-Geroge Airstrip, full of weapons, ammunition, and supplies. Very soon after, they attacked the nearby town of Lijnhaven, attacking police in the area and the first Gendarmerie responders. The Gendarmerie have setup roadblocks and have held a perimeter around the region, but aren't capable of a full urban assault. We have been hired to secure the town, assisting any surviving police and Gendarmerie forces, and to neutralize Pierre Ngunga. The True Sons have been broadcasting their attack on social media, so we have a good idea of where they are located within the town. We also know that they have taken several civilian hostages.
