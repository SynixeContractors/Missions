EMPLOYER Name: Integrated Economic Alliance Location: Tanoa While no IEA forces are directly present on Tanoa, they are economically and politically supporting the Tanoa's newly formed government as well as the local Gendarmerie. Since the Gendarmerie lack the resources, we have been hired to recapture a number of logistical sites that are still being controlled by mercenaries hired by the former corrupt government. ENEMY FORCES Name: Blackmire (Former) Location: Central Tanoa Hired by corrupt politicians of Tanoa's previous government, these former Blackmire mercenaries have seemingly refused to vacate some of the logistical sites they were once paid to protect. They have refused to cooperate with Tanoa's new officials, instead opting to bribe and threaten locals to keep control. Using their funds, they have been able to buy out a number of vulnerable young men and add them to their ranks. The former Blackmire members are known to use higher tier equipment and are well trained while the lower-ranking members use cheaper gear, lack training, and are less loyal to the cause. Name: Disenfranchised Young Men Location: Tanoa An ever present issue on the struggling island, Tanoa has a number of disenfranchised young men that have formed criminal gangs that conduct illicit activities such as smuggling drugs and weapons. While the old government would turn a blind eye for the right price, the new government wishes to crackdown on their activity. Many of these vulnerable young men have been hired by former Blackmire mercenaries on the island. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Gendarmerie Location: Horizon Islands The Gendarmerie are the police force of the Horizon Islands. Following the recent political changes, the Gendarmerie has been weakened. Those who support the new governemnt are low in number and others are easy to be persuaded by corrupt sources. The Gendarmerie has cordoned off the AO but does not have the strength to directly assist in any operations. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Tanoan Civilians Location: Tanoa Tanoan locals continue to try to live their lives across the whole island despite the recent changes. While most are supportive of the new government, many miss the stability (or appearance of stability) that the old governemnt provided. Locals try to avoid areas controlled by mercenaries, but sometimes it is not feasible to do so.
