EMPLOYER Name: Federal Government of Iraq The Iraqi government is once again battling an ISIS insurgency. ISIS has popped up through out the country side, gaining quick, but short lasting victories over the Federal government. ENEMY FORCES Name: ISIS Location: Al Salman Born again from the crucible of fire that is the ongoing hostilities in the Levant, ISIS has achieved several key victories over the Iraqi government, including at Al Salman. However, their hold over the city has been short lived, as their two initial victories quickly turned to hasty retreats. They are hoping this third capture of the city will be all that is needed. ISIS forces in the AO have been seen equipped with last generation small arms, shoulder mounted weapons, and the occasional light wheeled asset. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Iraqi Army Location: Abd al-Karim Qasim Air Base, and at the north city limites of Al Salaman The Iraqi Army has taken up positions just north of Salman. The forces deployed to this AO have been weakened by consecutive battles for the city.
