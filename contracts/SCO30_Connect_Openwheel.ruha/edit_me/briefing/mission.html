Thirty minutes before race time group of MRAPs and pickup trucks approached racetrack. They shot to disperse crowds and subsequently moved into the paddock and seized Mastor and confiscated all 3 of the red gokarts. The Police have set up roadblocks to the south and west and they believe the group has gone into hiding as they don't have a way to transport gokarts with the vehicles they possess. Locals are reporting all 3 MRAPs going east through Muurimaki, none going through Ruhanpera, and one going through Ulvila.
