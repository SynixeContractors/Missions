EMPLOYER Name: BAF Location: Mediterranean Island ENEMY FORCES Name: Third Way Party Location: Mediterranean Island Local movement based on restoring precolonial identity of the Island. They have been running on a platform removing british presence of the Island. Recently they formed an armed militia demanding government to step down. They are armed with recently stolen new and old surplus equipment from British forces as well as equipment smuggled in.
