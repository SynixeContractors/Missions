SRB forces have steadily been expanding their grasp in the region. After their violent push into the Eastern Desert, they have set their sights on Al Salman. The SRB have set up several forward positions on the western and southern outskirts of the city, bombarding the city with constant rocket artillery whilst more troops amass ahead of their expected assault. Synixe has been contracted by the Emirate of Al-Jatar to assist their neighbours by alleviating pressure on Al Salman. Intelligence suggests the SRB have active three artillery batteries south of Al Salman, destroying them should force the SRB to postpone their assault long enough for allies to organize an international response. It has also been confirmed the SRB lack any dedicated anti-aircraft systems or weapons. Contractors will be provided parachutes, two crates prepared for airdrop, a helicopter and crew to infiltrate behind SRB lines.
