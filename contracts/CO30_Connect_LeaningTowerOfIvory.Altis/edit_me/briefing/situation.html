EMPLOYER Name: Chris Sangheili Investment banker from Argana. He and his brother fled the country after their father got killed for supporting the Tura during first Arganan civil war. He became a banker, while his brother after short stint in Illanzian Foreign Legion, became a freedom fighter for various causes. He has now been captured by local cartel who wants to trade him for their own with AAF. Name: Altis Police They control the peace in a wartorn region. They placed many roadblocks and want to disrupt FIA operations behind their lines.
