EMPLOYER Name: Chris Sangheili Chris Sangheili is an investment banker from Argana. He and his brother fled the country after their father was killed for supporting the Tura during the First Arganan Civil War. Chris became a banker, while his brother, after a short stint in the Illanzian Foreign Legion, became a freedom fighter for various causes. His brother has now been captured by a local cartel, which wants to trade him for one of their own being held by the AAF. ENEMY FORCES Name: Entente Location: Southern Altis Well armed cartel led by Maurice Lu, now cooperating with AAF against the FIA. Name: Altis Armed Forces Decently well equipped force, majority of their forces continue north, to push FIA out of the forests and mountains. They are searching hills for small band east of Kavala. Name: Altis Police They control the peace in a wartorn region. They placed many roadblocks and want to disrupt FIA operations behind their lines. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Freedom Independence Army Beaten back, now hiding in the mountain and forest regions on western part of Altis CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilian life slowly going back to normal.
