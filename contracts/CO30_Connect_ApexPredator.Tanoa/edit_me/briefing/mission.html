About a week ago a delegation from Chernarus arrived on island of Tanoa. Three engineers from Chernogorsk Locomotive Plant, including deputy of the CTO - Simon Federov, are overseeing the delivery and testing of a prototype T-100 series tank shown at Arms Expo in Novigrad. Satellite pictures of the La Rochelle Aerodrome show unloading of one railgun turret and two hulls. The mountain testing, important to the Horizon Islands government is due to start. The endurance testing is being run in the Red Spring open pit mine, by maintenance platoon of 3rd Company of Alpini stationed at Comms Bravo. Accomodation of the foreign delegation from CLF is being provided by a local hotel nearby the airfield.
