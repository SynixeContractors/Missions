About a week ago a delegation from Chernarus arrived on island of Tanoa. Three engineers from Chernagorsk Locomotive Plant are overseeing the delivery and testing of a prototype T-100 series tank shown at Arms Expo in Novigrad. Satelite pictures of the La Rochelle Aerodrome show unloading of one railgun turret and two hulls. The mountain testing, important to the Horizon Islands government is due to start. The endurance testing is being run in the Red Spring open pit mine, by maintenance platoon of 3rd Company of Alpini stationed around Mount Tanoa. Accomodation of the foreign delegation from CLF is being provided by a local hotel nearby the airfield.
