With Asarta's ascension into the CSP alliance they have been inducted into the Peace and Stability program and their first task was supporting law enforcement operations against the Pruszków cartel in the Clara Islands. Asartan forces have deployed onto the archipelago and have staged their operations at the main airfield. They began systematically disrupting the ground operations of the cartel by patrolling the jungle and raiding houses of important players on the island. Synixe has been hired to strike against the peacekeeping operations and embarrass the newest member of the CSP. VIA wants to recover Janusz "Parasol" Prasol. Our employer believes the target should still be on site while Police conduct a search of that property. Additional targets are the Asarta-held airfield and Gendarmerie's HQ in Santa Isabel.
