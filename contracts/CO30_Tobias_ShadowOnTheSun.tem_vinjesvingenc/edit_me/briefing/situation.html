EMPLOYER Name: Virelian Intelligence Agency The VIA has arranged a safehouse for Synixe to operate from, and vehicles will also be provided to ensure a clandestine presence in Nyrland. ENEMY FORCES Name: The Kingdom of Nyrland Location: Vinjesvingen Vinjesvingen was a sparsely populated region in Nyrland. Due to a heightened military presence and the increase in weapons research and development, most civilians have left the region. The military occupies the airfield in the south, an FOB in the north-west, a weapons factory in Floten and a military police outpost in Myrbo. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Name: Nyrlanders Most, if not all civilians have left the region due to economic, health and safety concerns.
