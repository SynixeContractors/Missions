NATO forces are facing a delicate situation in Zargabad. Recent clashes pushed back Taliban forces, but Mullah Rahmat, the regional leader, remains elusive. Local sympathies lie heavily with the Taliban, hindering NATO's ability to openly search for him. NATO's embedded agent, Alex Adams, initially provided valuable intel on Taliban weapon caches and a key figure named Abu Ahmed operating out of Hazar Bagh. He is suspected of managing weapon caches delivered to the Taliban outpost in Hazar Bagh and using these weapons to mobilize new recruits. However, upon returning to Zargabad to deliver intel on potential checkpoint locations and Mullah Rahmat possibly hiding in the Abandoned Mosque, Adams went quiet, possibly captured. Our primary objective is to locate and capture Abu Ahmed in Hazar Bagh and Mullah Rahmat in Zargabad, disrupting Taliban weapon stockpiles and recruitment efforts. Additionally locate and potentially rescue Alex Adams, leveraging intel he might have provided before capture. Destroy the ammo caches in Hazar Bagh to further cripple Taliban operations. Things to remember: If you come face to face with Ahmed keep in mind that he knows every person that he has recruited, so take him down or apprehend him before he warns Rehmat about you. While entering the main district of Zargabad, approach from the Northern Route which leads to Abu Ahmed's hideout, it's a familiar path for Taliban reinforcements so they won't suspect anything and you'll have a crucial element of surprise. They won't know you're coming until it's too late.
