The Lingshan Islands are located in the South china Sea 400 km off the eastern coast of Philippines. Ever since the colonial days the islands have been under the control of the Dutch, with only a short period during Second Sino-Japanese War where they were under Japanese occupation. They have since become a common holiday destination for Chinese tourists. After successful action in recovery of key research mission, Synixe is once again called to help in Lingshan Islands. EMPLOYER Name: Netherlands Dutch parliament has authorized a miltiary action to retake island. With naval invasion preparation underway, the need for intervention from Netherlands Air Force was clear, however the threat of land based surface to air missiles is very high. Dutch special operations are on the way to eliminate the SAM battery in East Lingshan. ENEMY FORCES Name: North Korea Location: Lingshan Islands Elite units of North Korean army deployed to Lingshan Islands. They possess modern weapons and body armor. Most of the units are concentrated away from AO anticipating a naval invasion from the south. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians present in the area.
