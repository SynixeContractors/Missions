Few days ago a cyclone "Gabrielle" has hit the Fulao Islands, causing widespread damage. The less prepared Monyet Island has been mostly abandoned by the government and widespread looting has occured. This included raids by looters on the former garrison north east of airfield, where they captured few heavy machineguns and small arms left behind during evacuation. Returning to the island, police have been unable to reestablish peace and continue delivery of aid. They have been ambushed in the north and have encountered entranched enemy in Nirwana.
