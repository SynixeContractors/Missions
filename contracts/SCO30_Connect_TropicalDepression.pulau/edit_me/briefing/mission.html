Few days ago a cyclone "Gabrielle" hit the Fulao Islands, causing widespread damage. The less prepared Monyet Island has been mostly abandoned by the government, and widespread looting has occurred. This included raids by looters on the former garrison northeast of the airfield, where they captured a few heavy machine guns and small arms left behind during the evacuation. Returning to the island, police have been unable to reestablish peace and continue delivery of aid. They have been ambushed in the north and have encountered entrenched enemy in Nirwana.
