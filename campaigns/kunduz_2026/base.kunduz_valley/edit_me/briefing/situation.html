EMPLOYER Name: Integrated Economies Alliance (IEA) Location: HQ in Virelia Concerned about regional stability in Takistan, the IEA has contracted your unit to assist local forces in countering insurgent threats in the Wahat Nura Province. ENEMY FORCES Name: Ikhwan as-Sayf Location: Wahat Nura Province, Takistan A well-armed insurgent group operating in the Wahat Nura Valley, known for guerrilla tactics and ambushes. They are equipped with small arms, RPGs, and light vehicles. Their motive is to destabilize the region and undermine local governance. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Takistani Military Location: Various bases in Wahat Nura Province The Takistani Military has a small presence in the region, made up of local soldiers with limited training and equipment. They are cooperative but require guidance and support to effectively counter the insurgent threat. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Takistani Militia Location: Various locations in Wahat Nura Province The militia consists of local villagers and military deserters who have taken up arms to defend their villages. Their allegiances can be fluid, and they may either assist or hinder operations depending on the situation. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Many civilians in the Wahat Nura Valley are caught in the crossfire between insurgents and military forces. They may have family members involved with one or more of the factions. They may report on activities to any side.
