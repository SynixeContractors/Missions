After the latest elections in Korsac the losing side decided to take up arms in an attempt to topple the winning government and take power for themselves. Since the local defense forces are having trouble suppressing this rebellion, Synixe has been hired to perform COIN (Counter Insurgency) operations in the region in order to aid the winning government. Terrain considerations: Korsac is a mostly flat area that possesses a mix of lush forests and open plains. EMPLOYER Name: Korsac Government ENEMY FORCES Name: Korsac Separatists Interested in toppling the current government and gaining power. They posses a mix of modern and old Russian weaponry and opt for carrier rigs instead of vests. They use a myriad of vehicles and are known to have access to tanks and helicopters. FRIENDLY FORCES Name: Korsac Defense Force Location: Comms Ekho They use modern Russian weaponry and modular carriers for protection. They have a wide array of vehicles ranging from Tanks to Helicopters. INDEPENDENT FORCES Name: Korsac Armed Locals A group of locals not allied with either side and just interested in protecting their property. They carry old weapons they already had at their disposal. CIVILIAN CONSIDERATIONS Civilians are present throughout the AO.
